Dog

The dog was the first species to be domesticated.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Lifespan: 10 - 13 years
  • Smallest adult breed: Yorkshire Terrier
  • Largest adult breed: English Mastiff

Modern dog breeds show more variation in size, appearance, and behavior than any other domestic animal. Humans and dogs have a special relationship since they co-evolved with eachother. The relationship dates back at least 32,000 years.
